⏰ BOT SCHEDULE:

Current: 2:00 AM UTC (Daily)
Vietnam: 9:00 AM Vietnam time

Want to change the time?

Edit: .github/workflows/intelligence-update.yml

  schedule:
    - cron: '0 2 * * *'
                 ^ change this number

Examples:
  '0 0 * * *'  → Midnight UTC
  '0 6 * * *'  → 6 AM UTC (1 PM Vietnam)
  '0 12 * * *' → Noon UTC (7 PM Vietnam)
  '0 */6 * * *' → Every 6 hours

**Extend Scrapers:**
